The product insight
Spotify organizes music by sound.

But K-pop fans often organize their taste differently.


They remember generations. Eras. Concepts. Debut waves. The year a group changed its sound. The song that made them a fan.


This experiment explores what music discovery could look like when the interface is organized around the cultural context surrounding the music.
